# Free Temp Mail

![Free Temp Mail Banner](assets.preview.png)

**Free Temp Mail** is a modern, lightweight, and easy-to-use temporary email service. It allows users to generate disposable email addresses instantly, check their inbox in real-time, and copy or delete emails with a single click. Perfect for signing up for websites, testing forms, or protecting your personal email from spam.

---

## 🌟 Features

- **Instant Temp Email Generation:** Create a new temporary email address with one click.
- **Real-Time Inbox:** Polls inbox every 15 seconds to show incoming messages instantly.
- **Copy to Clipboard:** Easily copy your temporary email or message content with animation feedback.
- **Read/Unread Status:** Tracks messages you have opened for better inbox management.
- **Delete Email:** Clear your temporary account and messages securely.
- **Mobile-Friendly:** Responsive UI with smooth mobile menu toggle and scroll behavior.
- **Linkify URLs:** Automatically detects and links URLs in messages.
- **Secure:** No personal data is required; everything runs temporarily and locally.
